Privacy Policy

Campus Saga Mobile Application

Last updated: March 4, 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what information Campus Saga collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using the app you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly and data generated through your use of the app.

Account Information:

  • Email address or phone number used to register
  • Display name and profile photo (optional)
  • University affiliation and student ID (for verification purposes)

Content You Create:

  • Posts, comments, and votes you submit
  • Promotional materials submitted by authorized users
  • Verification images uploaded for student ID verification

Usage & Device Data:

  • Device type, OS version, and app version
  • Firebase Analytics events (screen views, feature interactions)
  • Firebase Crashlytics crash reports (anonymized)
  • Push notification tokens (FCM)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To display your posts and activity within the platform
  • To verify your student identity through campus ambassadors
  • To send relevant push notifications (new issues, resolutions, announcements)
  • To compute university rankings based on aggregated activity
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs using crash reports
  • To enforce community guidelines and prevent misuse

3. Anonymous Posting

Posts created by students are displayed anonymously to other users. However, your identity is stored internally so that campus administrators and app administrators can investigate abuse reports. Anonymity is not guaranteed in cases of legal obligations or serious community violations.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only in these circumstances:

  • Service providers: Firebase (Google), Cloudinary — used for authentication, storage, and media hosting.
  • University administrators: Issue content (not your identity) is visible to administrators of your own university.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose data if required by applicable law or a valid legal process.
  • Safety: To protect the rights and safety of users or the public.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data as long as your account is active. Posts and comments may remain on the platform after account deletion unless you explicitly request their removal. Verification images are deleted after the verification process is complete.

6. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including Firebase Authentication, Firestore security rules, and encrypted transport (HTTPS/TLS). No system is 100% secure; please use a strong password and keep your credentials private.

7. Children's Privacy

Campus Saga is intended for university students (18+). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us immediately.

8. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access & correction: You can view and edit your profile at any time in the app.
  • Account deletion: Use Settings → Delete Account to permanently remove your data.
  • Notification control: You can opt out of push notifications in your device settings.
  • Data portability: Contact us to request an export of your data.

9. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase – Auth, Firestore, Storage, Messaging, Analytics
  • Cloudinary – Media hosting
  • Google Sign-In – OAuth authentication

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above and, for significant changes, notify you via push notification or in-app message. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
